How to Scale Text

📱 Mobile & Tablet

Use pinch-to-zoom gestures directly on the song lyrics area to resize the chords and lyrics text. Other parts of the app remain unaffected.

🖥️ Desktop

Use the A+ / A- buttons at the bottom-left corner of the screen to increase or decrease the song text size.
