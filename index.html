How to Navigate

Pinch to Zoom (Mobile/Tablet)

Use two fingers to pinch in or out on the song sheet. Only chord & lyric text resizes — not the whole page.

A+ A−

A+ / A− Buttons (Desktop)

Use the floating buttons at the bottom-left to resize the chord and lyric text.

Swipe Left / Right

Swipe horizontally on the song to navigate to the next or previous song in the list.
