How to Read Songs

Mobile & Tablet

Use two-finger pinch-to-zoom gesture inside the song content area to resize lyrics and chords. Other parts of the app remain unaffected.

Desktop

Use the A+ and A- buttons at the bottom-left of the screen to increase or decrease the lyrics/chord font size.

Swipe Navigation

Swipe left or right to navigate to the next or previous song in the list.
